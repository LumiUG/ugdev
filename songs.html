THE Albums

Dark Blossom

"And I'll light fireworks with their blood."

Lexycat's magnum opus! This gets my imaginary prize for best album of 2024, and also another prize for making me replay the same songs forever.

They Know Who They Are

Most likely to be my favorite album of all time.
A masterpiece that portrays and speaks to you of the suffering that you may or may not see.

I will hold this close to my heart for a very, very long time.

Monarch of Monsters

An album full of raw emotion and anger. It hurts. It should hurt, as it's part of healing.

There most likely won't be another piece of media like this ever. Oh, and there's also a novella out there to read.

Plastic Death

The album that introduced me to "Glass Beach", and genuinely their best album aswell.

誰が為のエンドロール

The newest Chroma album, which *is* the best chiptune i've heard in a long while.

Metacontinues

Yep, yeah yup. That's a Frums album alright.

Techdog (1-7)

Longest album (albums?) i'll ever listen to. Filled with noise and long songs.

Carousel

The second-best vylet album. Sorry, that's just how it is!!

KID A MNESIA

"That there, that's not me"

Wetdream

A very emotional album, and angry in some sections...
